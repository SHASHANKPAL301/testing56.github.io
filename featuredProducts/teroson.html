TEROSON

Henkel Teroson is a renowned brand in the adhesive and sealant industry, known for its high-quality products and innovative solutions. The company is a division of Henkel AG & Co. KGaA, a German multinational company with a rich history dating back to 1876. Teroson's product range encompasses a wide variety of adhesives, sealants, coatings, and other chemical solutions that cater to numerous industries, including automotive, construction, electronics, aerospace, and more. Their products are widely used for bonding, sealing, and protecting materials, offering reliable and durable solutions to various industrial challenges. Henkel Teroson maintains a strong customer focus by providing technical support, training, and tailored solutions to address specific application needs. Their expertise and global presence have earned them a solid reputation as a trusted partner across industries worldwide.
