CUMI

Carborundum Universal Limited (CUMI) is a leading manufacturer and supplier of industrial ceramics, electro- minerals, and abrasive products based in India. Established in 1954, the company has grown to become one of the most trusted names in the abrasives and industrial materials industry. One of CUMI's primary product lines is abrasives, where they produce a wide array of grinding wheels, coated abrasives, and superabrasives. These products find extensive use in metal fabrication, precision grinding, and surface finishing applications. Their abrasive products are known for their high quality, durability, and efficiency, making them a preferred choice for many businesses worldwide. CUMI's commitment to research and development has played a crucial role in maintaining its competitive edge. The company continuously invests in innovation to develop new and advanced products that meet the evolving needs of its customers and adhere to international quality standards.
